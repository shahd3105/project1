About us

NASA (National Aeronautics and Space Administration)

One of the most prominent global agencies specialized in space exploration and aeronautics

Founded in 1958

With the aim of expanding the boundaries of human knowledge through the study of the universe

NASA is famous

By sending astronauts to the moon, exploring planets like Mars, and launching space telescopes like the Hubble Telescope

Why should we fund NASA?

NASA's unique mission provides benefits large and small.

  • Money spent on space exploration creates jobs, stimulates businesses, and grows the economy.
  • Our innovations also improve everyday life.
  • It provides madical research and supports disaster respose.
